' Our Heads '

Future rests on the decisions you make today. A good school education should be of prime importance as it ensures the development of holistic personality. The academic programs here, are carefully designed and customized to create value for the students and to address their diverse needs. This institution has the distinction of being involved in large, complex and high quality consulting projects and researches. It grooms students to strive for excellence in various spheres enabling them to tackle the increasingly competitive global environment. This school was established by my mother Mrs. Myrtle J Singh, an educationist with a vision. In 60 years of its existence it has emerged as a leading school in Jaipur with ISO certification; preparing students for ICSE (Year 10) and ISC (Year 12) Examinations. The achievements, awards and accolades won by the school stand testimony to the sincere efforts of the staff and the management. I extend a personal invitation to parents and students to consider the rewards of being part of Children’s Academy.

Dr. Bharat D Singh CEO

Life is an unending journey. For us, this journey of excellence at Children’s Academy is perennial. A gratifying odyssey that began in 1961, with the vision of holistic and globally relevant education to the young citizens of India, has now become a benchmark in helping generations of students to dream, visualize and plan for their future. At Children’s Academy, we believe that education is a process of awakening individual potential to creative knowledge. In this fast pacing world, as educators we need to understand the importance of imparting quality education to our inmates including a strong component of cultural values, environment awareness and social responsibility. We also believe that each child is like an atom that holds tremendous power within to serve as agents of change and thus are confident that our students, enriched with a sense of high morality and social responsibility will be the makers of a virtuous society.

Ms Hemant Kachhawa Principal - Nirman Nagar

The goal of education should be to provide complex learning environment for students which incorporates authentic learning, assessing and personal development. At Children's Academy, we make all efforts to instill core competencies and skills in our students to be leaders in this world of 21st century. Along with giving an exposure to the students to develop aptitude and proficiency, quintessential values like loyalty, integrity and of course a sense of discipline is also instilled in them. Leaving behind a golden past, we are inching towards a glittering future each day in the field of sports, academics as well as co-curricular activities. We are proud to have students participating in various events at the state as well as national level. In addition, our rankings in the Olympiads have been excellent. We have striven to maintain an excellent 100% result in the Board Examinations both at the ICSE and ISC level. In all these years what has remained unchanged is the zeal and spirit to achieve and to excel. Committed to the grand theme in the words of our CEO-Dr. Bharat D. Singh – ANOTHER WORLD IS POSSIBLE.

Dr. (Mrs.) Deepanjali Gardner Principal – Banipark

' About US '

VISION & MISSION:

“To give every child the ability to thrive in, and to contribute to a changing world.”

We believe the role of a school is to continually improve itself, bringing together the best ideas in education, to help children fulfill their full potential. The goal is to nurture well-rounded, free-thinking, capable individuals who are rooted in humanistic values of caring for their fellow-beings. Individuals, who will not only achieve personal success, but will see the value in improving the world around them.
